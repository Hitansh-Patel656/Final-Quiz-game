What are Quiz Games?

Quiz games put your mind to the test on various subjects. Some quizzes test your ability to 
think logically and creatively,whereas others may challenge your knowledge of world events.
Types of Quiz Games:
1. Trivia Quizzes : General knowledge questions on various topics.
2.Educational Quizzes : Designed for learning and improving skills.
3.Puzzle-Based Quizzes : Brain teasers, riddles, and logic puzzles.
4.Speed Quizzes : Answer quickly before time runs out.
5.Live Quiz Shows : Interactive online or TV-based quizzes.
